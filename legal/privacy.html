This Privacy Policy describes how Jolene Casko ("I", "me", "my") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you visit jolene.taurustech.me (the "Site") or use my services.

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit the Site, I may collect:

I use the following third-party services that may collect information:

Cookies

This Site uses minimal cookies for essential functionality only. I do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies. For more information, see the Cookie Policy.

Data Security

I implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information. However, no internet transmission is completely secure. Payment information is processed securely through Stripe and is never stored on my servers.

Your Rights
