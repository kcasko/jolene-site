How It Works

I work with musicians on album art, singles, and visual branding. I also take on personal commissions, editorial projects, and conceptual pieces.

Every project is different—scope, timeline, and pricing depend on what you're looking for. I don't use fixed packages because I'd rather build something specific to your needs than fit you into a template.

Fill out the form below with as much detail as you can. I'll review your submission and get back to you within 2-3 business days to discuss your project, timeline, and pricing.

What Happens Next?

1. Review: I'll review your submission within 2-3 business days.

2. Discussion: If it's a good fit, I'll email you to discuss details, timeline, and provide a formal quote.

3. Agreement: Once we agree on scope and pricing, I'll send a project agreement and invoice for the 50% deposit.

4. Creation: After deposit is received, I'll begin work and share drafts for feedback as we go.

5. Delivery: Final files delivered upon completion and final payment.
