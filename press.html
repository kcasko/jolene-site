About Jolene Casko

Jolene Casko is a Pennsylvania-based digital collage artist specializing in surreal visual narratives inspired by music, poetry, and emotion. With over 20 years of experience creating layered, dreamlike compositions, she has established herself as a sought-after album art designer for musicians across genres.

Her work blends vintage imagery, celestial elements, and nature motifs to create pieces that feel both nostalgic and otherworldly. Since transitioning to full-time art in 2022, she has expanded into NFT collections and continues to collaborate with musicians, brands, and collectors worldwide.
