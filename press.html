Writing & Features

Jolene's work and voice have been featured across literary platforms, editorial sites, and online art communities.

Featured Platforms and Contributions

SmokeLong Quarterly

Jolene is a featured artist and contributor at SmokeLong Quarterly, a respected literary platform. Her artist bio highlights her role as a digital collage artist from Pennsylvania, active in freelance work since 2015. The platform showcases her surreal visual language alongside literary content, connecting her imagery with narrative-driven audiences.
