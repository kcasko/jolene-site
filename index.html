Where Forgotten Images Find New Life

Jolene's work features celestial motifs, feminine figures in transformation, and landscapes that blur the line between memory and fantasy. Each piece encourages viewers to pause, imagine, and construct their own narratives from the fragments she assembles.

Working primarily in digital media, she sources vintage photos, archival illustrations, and public-domain imagery, then remixes them into new contexts through layering, color grading, and textural unity. Her technique involves assembling multiple image fragments into unified scenes, using surreal juxtapositions that feel cohesive rather than chaotic.

Her surreal collage aesthetic remains consistent across every medium, whether she's designing for clients, minting digital collectibles, or offering museum-quality metal prints.
